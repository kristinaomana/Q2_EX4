Shawarma

Shawarma is a popular Middle Eastern dish. It is made from marinated meat such as beef, chicken and lamb. Shawarma can be mixed with different vegetables and served with rice or fries.



Pasta

Pasta is a type of food made from water, eggs, and flour. There are many types of pasta like Spaghetti, Carbonara, Aglio Olio, Pesto, etc. There are also many different pasta shapes like fettucine, macaroni etc.
